NgVas


Angular directive for a Visual Analog Scale (VAS)

What is NgVAS?

NgVAS is a AngularJS directive for the Visual Analog Scale (VAS). The VAS is widely used measuring tool to get responses to questions on a continous scale. HCI research has shown that the VAS have superior accuracy than using sliders as the VAS is not being biased by having a starting postion [Funke 2015].

There have also been studies comparing continous scales (e.g. sliders and VAS) and categorical scales, such as radio boxes [Cook 2001]. VAS are widely used by medical reserchers to get pain feedbacks from their patients [google scholar].
